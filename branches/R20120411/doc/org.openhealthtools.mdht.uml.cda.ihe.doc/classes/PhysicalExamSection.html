Physical Exam Section

[Section: templateId 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.1.9.15]

The physical exam section shall contain only the required and optional subsections performed.

  1. SHALL conform to Physical Exam Narrative Section template (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.24)
  2. MAY contain zero or one [0..1] component, such that
    1. Contains exactly one [1..1] Vital Signs Section (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.25)
