Surgeries Section

[Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.7.1]

This section defines all interventional, surgical, diagnostic, or therapeutic procedures or treatments pertinent to the patient historically at the time the document is generated. The section may contain all procedures for the period of time being summarized, but should include notable procedures.

The list of surgeries section shall contain a narrative description of the diagnostic and therapeutic operative procedures and associated anesthetic techniques the patient received in the past.

The list of surgeries section shall include entries for procedures and references to procedure reports when known as described in the Entry Content Modules.

  1. SHALL conform to Surgeries Narrative Section template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.7)
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code/@code="47519-4" History of procedures (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC) (CONF-423, CONF-424)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] title (CONF-425)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] text (CONF-422)
  5. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] entry, such that
    1. Contains exactly one [1..1] External Reference (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.4.4)
  6. Contains at least one [1..*] entry, such that
    1. Contains exactly one [1..1] Procedure Activity Procedure (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.14)
  7. SHOULD satisfy: title is valued with a case-insensitive language-insensitive text string containing "procedures". (CONF-426)
  8. SHOULD satisfy: include one or more of the following: ProcedureActivityAct, ProcedureActivityObservation, ProcedureActivityProcedure (CONF-422)
