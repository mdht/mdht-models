Tuberculosis Socio Behavioral Boolean Risk Factor Observation

[Observation: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.15.3.15]

  1. SHALL conform to CCD Social History Observation template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.33)
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @negationInd (CONF:874)
  3. SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:872)
  4. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] value, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet 2.16.840.1.114222.4.11.3228 Sociobehavioral risk factor STATIC 1, where its data type is CD (CONF:873)
  5. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code/@code="ASSERTION" (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.4 HL7ActCode)
