Patient Awareness

[Participant2: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.48]

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="SBJ" (CONF-181)
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] awarenessCode (CONF-182)
  3. Patient awareness SHALL contain exactly one participant / participantRole / id, which SHALL have exactly one value, which SHALL also be present in ClinicalDocument / recordTarget / patientRole / id. (CONF-183)
    • [OCL]: self.participantRole.id->one(id : datatypes::II |
  not id.root.oclIsUndefined())
