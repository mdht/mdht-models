Tuberculosis Relevant Medical Condition Problem Observation

[Observation: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.15.3.108]

  1. SHALL conform to CCD Problem Observation template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.28)
  2. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code/@code="64572001" Condition (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.96 SNOMEDCT) (CONF-159)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] value, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Relevant Medical Conditions STATIC, where its data type is CE
