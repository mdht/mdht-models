General Header Constraints

[ClinicalDocument: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.1.1]

Known Subclasses: Summary Of Care Record, Clinical Office Visit Summary, View Download Transmit Summary

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId ( ) such that it
    1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.1.1"
  2. SHALL conform to Consol General Header Constraints template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.1.1)
  3. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] recordTarget
    1. Such recordTargets SHALL conform to Consol Record Target
    2. Such recordTargets SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] patientRole
      1. This patientRole SHALL conform to Consol Patient Role
      2. This patientRole SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] patient
        1. This patient SHALL conform to Consol Patient
        2. This patient SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] ethnicGroupCode, where the @code SHALL be selected from ValueSet Ethnicity Category ValueSet STATIC
        3. This patient SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] raceCode, where the @code SHALL be selected from ValueSet Race Category ValueSet STATIC
        4. This patient SHOULD contain zero or more [0..*] languageCommunication
          1. Such languageCommunications SHALL conform to Consol Language Communication
          2. Such languageCommunications SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] languageCode, where the @code SHALL be selected from ValueSet LanguageCode STATIC
  4. SHALL have at least one documentationOf/serviceEvent/performer OR have at least one componentOf/encompassingEncounter/responsibleParty OR have at least one componentOf/encompassingEncounter/encounterParticipant
