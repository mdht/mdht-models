Problem Concern Entry

[Act: templateId 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.4.5.2]

This entry is a specialization of the Concern Entry, wherein the subject of the concern is focused on a problem.

  1. SHALL conform to Concern Entry template (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.4.5.1)
  2. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] entryRelationship, such that
    1. Contains @typeCode="SUBJ" SUBJ (has subject)
    2. Contains exactly one [1..1] Problem Entry (templateId: 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.4.5)
