Vital Signs Section

[Section: templateId 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.25]

The vital signs section shall contain a narrative description of the measurement results of a patient's vital signs.

  1. SHALL conform to CCD Vital Signs Section template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.16) (6.3.3.4.4.1)
