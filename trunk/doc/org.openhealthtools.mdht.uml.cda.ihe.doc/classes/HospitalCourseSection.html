Hospital Course Section

[Section: templateId 1.3.6.1.4.1.19376.1.5.3.1.3.5]

The hospital course section shall contain a narrative description of the sequence of events from admission to discharge in a hospital facility.

  1. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code/@code="8648-8" HOSPITAL COURSE (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.6.1 LOINC)
