Plan Of Care Activity Supply

[Supply: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.1.25]

  1. Extends Plan Of Care Activity
  2. Contains exactly one [1..1] @classCode="SPLY" with data type ActClassSupply (CodeSystem: 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 HL7ActClass)
  3. SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode (CONF-487)
    1. SHALL satisfy: moodCodeValue (CONF-489)
  4. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF-486)
  5. SHALL satisfy: moodCodeValue (CONF-489)
  6. SHALL contain one or more sources of information (CONF-491)
