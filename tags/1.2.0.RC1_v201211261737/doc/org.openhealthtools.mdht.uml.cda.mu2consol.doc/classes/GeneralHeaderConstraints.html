General Header Constraints

[ClinicalDocument: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.1.1]

Known Subclasses: Summary Of Care Record, Clinical Office Visit Summary, View Download Transmit Summary

INLINE

  1. SHALL conform to Consol General Header Constraints template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.1.1)
  2. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] recordTarget
    1. Such recordTargets SHALL conform to Consol Record Target
    2. Such recordTargets SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] patientRole
      1. This patientRole SHALL conform to Consol Patient Role
      2. This patientRole SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] patient
        1. This patient SHALL conform to Consol Patient
        2. This patient SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] ethnicGroupCode, where the @code SHALL be selected from ValueSet Ethnicity Category ValueSet STATIC
        3. This patient SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] raceCode, where the @code SHALL be selected from ValueSet Race Category ValueSet STATIC
        4. This patient SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] languageCommunication
          1. This languageCommunication SHALL conform to Consol Language Communication
          2. This languageCommunication SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] languageCode, where the @code SHALL be selected from ValueSet LanguageCode STATIC (CONF:5407)
  3. SHALL contain at least one [1..*] documentationOf
    1. Such documentationOfs SHALL conform to Consol Documentation Of
    2. Such documentationOfs SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] serviceEvent (CONF:14836)
      1. This serviceEvent SHALL conform to Consol Service Event
      2. This serviceEvent SHALL contain at least one [1..*] performer
        1. Such performers SHALL conform to Consol Performer1
        2. Such performers SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedEntity
          1. This assignedEntity SHALL conform to Consol Assigned Entity
          2. This assignedEntity SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedPerson, where its type is CDA Person
            1. Contains exactly one [1..1] CDA Person
  4. Contains zero or one [0..1] componentOf
    1. This componentOf SHALL conform to Consol Component Of
    2. This componentOf SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] encompassingEncounter (CONF:9956)
      1. This encompassingEncounter SHALL conform to Consol Encompassing Encounter
      2. This encompassingEncounter SHALL contain at least one [1..*] encounterParticipant, where its type is CDA Encounter Participant
        1. Contains exactly one [1..1] CDA Encounter Participant
